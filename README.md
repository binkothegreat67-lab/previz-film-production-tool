## What It Is

Pre-Viz™ is a single-file HTML film pre-visualization tool built as a free, open-source alternative to expensive industry software like Shot Designer. It runs entirely in the browser — no installation, no account, no subscription. Open the file and work.

Designed for film students, educators, and independent filmmakers anywhere in the world — from Los Angeles to Lima to Lagos to Windhoek.

---

## Why It Exists

Professional pre-visualization tools cost hundreds of dollars per year. Film students in underfunded programs, community colleges, and schools in the Global South don't have that. Pre-Viz™ was built on a single non-negotiable design constraint: **equity**. Every architectural decision — offline-first, single-file, browser-native, multilingual, zero cost — flows from that mission.

---

## Features

- **Interactive Floor Plan** — drag-and-drop camera, actor, and lighting placement on a scaled grid (ft/m)
- **Director's Monitor** — real-time simulated viewfinder with live exposure and lens data
- **3-Point Lighting System** — Key, Fill, and Back lights with color temperature, intensity, and ratio display
- **Exposure Engine** — f-stop, ISO, shutter speed, 180° rule enforcement, aperture simulation
- **Lux AI Teaching Assistant** — built-in Socratic AI tutor powered by Claude (Anthropic), designed to teach — not just answer
- **Shot Data Strips** — exportable CAM / EXP / LGT / TLT production data lines
- **Print & PDF Export** — floor plan + monitor view formatted for production binders
- **Multilingual** — English, Spanish, French, Portuguese (native, not translated after the fact)
- **Android-first** — optimized for mid-range Samsung Galaxy devices with S Pen support
- **Offline-capable** — works without internet after first load

---

## Technology

Single-file HTML/CSS/JavaScript architecture. No framework. No build step. No dependencies to install.

- **Plotly.js** — floor plan rendering
- **Anthropic API** — Lux AI teaching assistant (Claude Sonnet)
- **Web Speech API** — voice layer (roadmap)
- **Browser-native** — zero server infrastructure required
